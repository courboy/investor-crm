Key Market Observations

Yield Compression

Initial yields averaging 4.6% market-wide. Prime infill assets trading at 2.7-3.6%. Outer markets offering 100-150bps premium at 4.5-5.7%.

Offshore Capital Dominance

Offshore direct buyers (KKR, Blackstone, LaSalle) and offshore-backed funds (OTPP, PGIM, BGO) account for 60%+ of transaction volume.

Development Land Premium

Development sites trading at $296-$1,375/sqm. Badgerys Creek (airport precinct) at $296/sqm reflects strategic positioning for future demand.

Infill vs Outer Spread

Infill assets command ~40% premium on $/sqm GLA but yields ~80bps tighter. Best risk-adjusted returns in Outer markets with strong covenants.

REIT Capital Recycling

Goodman, Stockland, Dexus actively divesting to institutional buyers. Clearing mature assets to fund development pipelines.

WALE Sweet Spot

Longest WALEs (20+ years) trade at tightest yields but limit upside. Mid-term WALEs (3-5 years) offer reversion potential with manageable risk.
