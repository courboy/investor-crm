📊 Key Market Insights

Yield Compression Continues

Initial yields averaging 4.6% across the market, with prime infill assets trading at 2.7-3.6%. Outer markets offering 100-150bps premium at 4.5-5.7%.

Offshore Capital Dominant

Offshore direct buyers (KKR, Blackstone, LaSalle) and offshore-backed funds (OTPP, PGIM, BGO) account for 60%+ of transaction volume by value.

Development Land Premium

Development sites trading at $296-$1,375/sqm — Badgerys Creek (airport precinct) at $296/sqm represents strategic positioning for future logistics demand.

Infill vs Outer Spread

Infill assets command ~40% premium on $/sqm GLA ($5,200 vs $3,200) but yields ~80bps tighter. Best risk-adjusted returns in Outer markets with strong tenant covenants.

REITs Recycling Capital

Goodman, Stockland, Dexus actively divesting to institutional buyers — clearing mature assets to fund development pipelines. Buying opportunity for value-add investors.

WALE Sweet Spot: 3-5 Years

Longest WALEs (20+ years) trade at tightest yields but limit upside. Mid-term WALEs (3-5 years) offer reversion potential with manageable lease-up risk.
